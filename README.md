# Vrinda Store Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard Project)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the annual sales performance of **Vrinda Store** using Microsoft Excel.  
The goal of the analysis is to understand customer purchasing behavior, sales distribution across different states, order channels, and overall business performance.

The project uses Excel features such as **data cleaning, pivot tables, charts, and dashboard creation** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains sales transaction records including:

- Order ID
- Order Date
- Customer Gender
- Customer Age Group
- Sales Amount
- Order Status
- Sales Channel
- Product Category
- State

These fields were used to analyze sales patterns and build the dashboard.

---

## Business Questions Solved

This project answers several important business questions:

1. What is the monthly trend of **orders vs sales**?
2. Do **men or women contribute more to sales**?
3. What percentage of orders are **delivered, cancelled, or returned**?
4. Which **states generate the highest revenue**?
5. Which **age group contributes the most orders**?
6. Which **sales channels drive the most orders**?

---

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Orders vs Sales Trend
Analyzed the relationship between number of orders and revenue generated each month to identify seasonal patterns.

### 2. Men vs Women Sales
Compared purchasing behavior between male and female customers.

### 3. Order Status Analysis
Analyzed order completion rate including delivered, cancelled, refunded, and returned orders.

### 4. Sales by Top 5 States
Identified states that generate the highest sales revenue.

### 5. Age vs Gender Orders
Analyzed which age group contributes most to total orders.

### 6. Orders by Channel
Examined sales distribution across major e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, and others.

## Key Insights

- Women customers contributed **64% of total sales**.
- The majority of orders were **successfully delivered (92%)**.
- **Amazon generated the highest share of orders (~35%)**.
- **Maharashtra was the top revenue generating state**.
- The **adult age group contributed the highest number of orders**.

These insights help businesses understand their customer base and optimize sales strategies.
